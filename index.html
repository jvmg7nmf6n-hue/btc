▲ Market UPAR (Uptrend)

  • MA/EMA + BOS↑ — trend direction confirm.
  • Fib + FVG — pullback/dip pe entry zone (trend ke saath).
  • Risk Calculator — har entry pe stop + size.
  • Bachein: top pe FOMO chase; overbought RSI pe naya long.

▼ Market NEECHE (Downtrend)

  • BOS↓ — lower-low structure confirm.
  • Vol Risk Meter — leverage kam, capital bachao.
  • Counter-trend long avoid; trade to chhota + tez.
  • Bounces aksar bik jaate hain — confirm ke baghair na pakdein.

▬ RANGE / Sideways

  • Bollinger + S/R levels — range ke kinare.
  • Kinare se fade, beech mein avoid.
  • Breakout ka wait — confirm hone tak patience.
  • Confluence/MTF pe is waqt kam bharosa (whipsaw).

⚡ HIGH Volatility

  • Volatility Risk Meter — sab se pehle yeh.
  • Leverage ghatao, size chhoti.
  • Stop thoda wider (warna ek candle liquidate kar de).
  • Jaldbaazi nahi — saaf setup ka wait.

Dos & Don'ts · Har Tool TAP TO EXPAND
Har tool par tap karein — kab use karna, kya karna (✓) aur kya nahi (✗).
Risk Calculator — har trade ki buniyaad
Kab: HAR trade se pehle, bina kisi exception.
✓ Pehle risk% (1–2%) fix karo, phir us se position size nikalo.
✗ Pehle size/leverage socho phir risk — yeh ulta aur khatarnak.
FVG — imbalance/entry zone
Kab: trend ke saath pullback/entry zone dhoondte waqt.
✓ Trend ki direction mein FVG ko support/resistance zone maano + confirmation.
✗ Akele FVG par trade — fill ki koi guarantee nahi.
Confluence Score — signal ki strength
Kab: entry se theek pehle.
✓ 4+/5 alignment ka wait karo — fakeout risk kam.
✗ 2–3/5 (mixed) par zabardasti entry.
MTF Trend — direction
Kab: trade ki direction decide karte waqt.
✓ Teenon timeframe ek taraf hon to bharosa zyada.
✗ Bade timeframe ke khilaaf chhote timeframe par trade.
Trend & Vol Regime — mahaul
Kab: session shuru karte waqt, strategy chunne se pehle.
✓ Strategy ko regime se match karo (trend mein trend-following, range mein range).
✗ Range mein trend-strategy ya high-vol mein bari size.
Volatility Risk Meter — garmi
Kab: leverage set karte waqt, khaas kar high-vol mein.
✓ ELEVATED/EXTREME par leverage ghatao, stop wider.
✗ High-vol mein high leverage — ek hi candle liquidate kar deti hai.
Bollinger Bands — volatility/range
Kab: range aur volatility samajhne ke liye.
✓ Squeeze (bands tang) = bara move aane wala, taiyar raho.
✗ Band touch ko akela buy/sell signal samajhna.
BOS — structure
Kab: trend jari/badalne ki tasdeeq.
✓ BOS ke baad pullback ka wait, phir entry.
✗ Har chhoti break ko BOS samajh lena.
Fib — pullback levels
Kab: pullback entry ya target zones ke liye.
✓ 0.5–0.618 zone + koi aur confluence.
✗ Akele Fib line par aankh band kar ke entry.
Opportunity Screener — kahan strength
Kab: dekhne ke liye abhi relative strength kahan hai.
✓ Top-ranked ko shortlist karo, phir khud chart + risk check.
✗ Score dekh kar seedha buy — yeh screener hai, signal nahi.
Trading Journal — asli edge
Kab: HAR trade ke baad (jeet ya haar).
✓ Why + lesson dono likho — isi se patterns nikalte hain.
✗ Sirf jeetne wale trades log karna (data jhoota ho jata hai).
Opportunity Screener RELATIVE STRENGTH · NOT A PREDICTION
Saare coins ko live 1h data par momentum + trend se rank karta hai. Yeh buy-list nahi aur koi prediction nahi — sirf ek screener jo dikhata hai abhi relative strength kahan hai. Entry se pehle khud chart + risk check zaroori.

Coin Groups · Definitions & Selection

Coins ko unke size, maqsad aur risk ke hisaab se teen groups mein baanta jata hai. Har group mein top 5 ka intikhab in 5 cheezon par hota hai:

Market Cap — total size (price × circulating supply) Volume — 24h kitna trade hua Liquidity — bina price hilaye khareed/bech Holders — kitne log rakhte hain Distribution — coins phaile hue ya chand wallets mein

🏛️ Majors LOW-ER RISK

Sab se bade market cap, sab se zyada liquid aur widely-held. Distribution behtar (kisi ek ka control nahi). Crypto ke "blue-chip".

Top 5: BTC · ETH · XRP · SOL · ADA
Risk: relatively kam · Volatility: kam

⚙️ Alt Coins MEDIUM RISK

"Alternative coins" — technically BTC ke ilawa sab. Amal mein: real utility wale established projects (oracles, smart contracts, interoperability) jo majors se chhote hain.

Top 5: LINK · LTC · AVAX · NEAR · DOT
Risk: darmiyani · Volatility: darmiyani-zyada

🐶 Meme Coins HIGH RISK

Internet culture/mazaq se bane — koi bunyaadi utility nahi. Value sirf community hype aur attention se. Distribution aksar kharab (chand bade wallets ke paas bara hissa).

Top 5: DOGE · SHIB · PEPE · BONK · WIF
Risk: bohot zyada · Volatility: extreme
💡 Farq ek nazar mein: Majors → bara cap, kam risk, store-of-value/blue-chip. Alts → utility wale, medium cap aur risk. Memes → hype-driven, no utility, sab se zyada risk aur volatility. Aam tor par jitna chhota cap, utna zyada risk aur potential move (dono taraf).
